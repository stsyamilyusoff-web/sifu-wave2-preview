Notes for Hafiz (Wave 2)
IC + full name are locked after verification (no self-edit). A change routes to admin re-review or a systematic re-check, your call which.
Short steps with white space are intentional (Apple HIG + completion data); we do not pad them with fields.
Open: Face ID sign-in is a returning-user feature, recommended for a later login pass (or a one-screen opt-in after first login), not the verification on-ramp.
Out of scope here, on purpose: the tutor PROFILE (self-view + the parent-facing showcase) and a post-class parent rating + review system are their own pitch and wave, coming next. This brief is the Wave 2 verification on-ramp only.
