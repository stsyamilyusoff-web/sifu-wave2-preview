The gate holds what proves a tutor is legit and qualified: identity, qualifications, the documents the admin reviews, and a short video that proves it is really them (a liveness check) and that parents watch to choose. Four steps to verified, down from seven (consolidation audit, 24 June). The friction fix on Bio stays: the IC age check fires the instant the IC is typed.

Out of scope here, on purpose: the tutor PROFILE (self-view + the parent-facing showcase) and a post-class parent rating + review system are their own pitch and wave, coming next. This brief is the Wave 2 verification on-ramp only.
