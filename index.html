Editing other verification items (documents, video) after approval triggers a light admin re-review.
Out of scope here, on purpose: the tutor PROFILE (self-view + the parent-facing showcase) and a post-class parent rating + review system are their own pitch and wave, coming next. This brief is the Wave 2 verification on-ramp only.
